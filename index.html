Brief Bio

I am a research scientist with Intel Labs, Hillsboro, OR. I obtained my Ph.D. in electrical and computer engineering from Georgia Tech in 2018. My other degrees include a B.E. in information engineering from Southeast University and a M.A.Sc in electrical engineering from the University of Victoria in 2012 and 2015, respectively.

My general research interests include wireless communications, signal processing, optimization, and machine learning. The current emphasis is on vehicular communications and networking, massive and millimeter wave MIMO systems, and machine learning for wireless communications.
